About Computational and Data Science

Dr Lakshmi Kanthan Narayanan - Head of Department

Dr Lakshmi Kanthan Narayanan is working as Associate Professor, in the Department of Computational Data Science - SIMATS Engineering. He is having more than twelve Years of Experience in Academia,Industry and Research. He currently guiding four Doctoral Scholars and he has guided more than hundred UG and PG scholars. He has published more than 35 articles and filled five patents. He has also authored three Engineering Text Books. Professor Kanthan is a dynamic researcher in the futuristic domains of Computer Science and Engineering such as IoT, IoV, IIoT, Gen-AI, Smart City Development, Cyber Security etc. He is also Editorial Board Reviewer for IGI Global, Springer and Elseiver Journals.
